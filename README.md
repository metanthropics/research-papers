## Overview

This repository serves as a public archive for research papers, technical reports, and white papers authored by Ekjot Singh and collaborators at Metanthropic Lab.

The goal is to provide a stable, accessible location for our written research artifacts, supplementing official publication venues or pre-print servers like arXiv when applicable.

## Papers

* **Revisiting AlexNet: Achieving High-Accuracy on CIFAR-10 with Modern Optimization Techniques** (2025)
    * Author: Ekjot Singh
    * Abstract: We revisit the original AlexNet architecture, adapting it for CIFAR-10 and achieving 95.7% accuracy by incorporating modern techniques like Batch Normalization, Adam optimizer, and advanced regularization.
    * [**Read PDF**](Revisiting_AlexNet__Achieving_High_Accuracy_on_CIFAR_10_with_Modern_Optimization_Techniques.pdf)
    * Associated Code: [metanthropics/revisiting-alexnet](https://github.com/metanthropics/revisiting-alexnet)
